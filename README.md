# Uber_Analysis_powerBI

This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of Uber ride performance, revenue trends, and driver/rider metrics. The dashboard is designed with a modern, "app-like" interface to allow stakeholders to navigate complex datasets effortlessly and extract actionable insights.

The goal was to simulate a real-world business intelligence environment where data drives decisions regarding fleet management, surge pricing, and geographic expansion.

Multi-Page Navigation: Interactive sidebar and home screen for seamless transitions between Overview, Vehicle, Revenue, Rider, and Location views.

Dynamic KPIs: Real-time tracking of Total Revenue ($13M), Completed vs. Cancelled Bookings, and Total Distance covered.

Vehicle Segment Analysis: Deep dives into performance metrics for Auto, Moto, Go Mini, Go Sedan, Premier, and Uber XL.

Geospatial Insights: Heatmaps and rankings of top Pickup and Drop-off locations.

Customer Sentiment: Analysis of Average Customer and Driver ratings to monitor service quality.

Operational Efficiency: Breakdown of "Cancel Reasons" and "Booking Trends" by month and quarter.

Rider/Driver: Behavioral analysis, retention, and rating distributions.

üìà Key Insights from the Data
Revenue Leaders: While Uber XL generates higher per-ride revenue, the "Auto" and "Go Mini" segments drive the highest volume of bookings.

Payment Preferences: A significant portion of transactions are moving toward digital (UPI/Wallet), though cash remains a vital payment method in specific zones.

Cancellation Trends: Identified peak hours where "Driver Not Found" or "Customer-initiated" cancellations spike, suggesting a need for increased driver incentives during those windows.
